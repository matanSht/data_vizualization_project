Introduction

The chip fabrication industry is in demand for very high accuracy of process control. The latest chip node technology (currenly in R&D phase) is trying to shrink the transistor dimentions to 3nm scale, and by that increase the amount of transistors on chip, which in turn lead to bigger memory capacity or more flexible and faster processors.

The manufacturers are facing tough challenges when fabricating chips in nanometric scales. Each chip need to provide the same (more or less) performances in terms of power comsumption, working frequency and more. One of the key enablers to meet such demands is metrology, sencitive to nanometric changes, as part of the process control.
